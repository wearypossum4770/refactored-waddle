Given a table invoice_items with the following structure:

create table invoice_items ( inv_num integer not null, item varchar(10) not null, price integer not null );

write an SQL query that returns a list of invoices (inv_num) with the total price of each (sum). The list should be ordered by inv_num (in descending order).

Examples:

1. Given:

inv_num | item | price --------+------+------ 3 | a | 10 3 | b | 15 1 | c | 7

your query should return:

inv_num | sum --------+---- 3 | 25 1 | 7

2. Given:

inv_num | item | price --------+------+------ 10 | A | 13 11 | C | 20 11 | D | 4 12 | D | 4 12 | D | 4

your query should return:

inv_num | sum --------+---- 12 | 8 11 | 24 10 | 13

my solution SELECT inv_num, Sum(price) as sum from invoice_items group by inv_num order by inv_num desc;
